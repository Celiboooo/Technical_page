Ingredients and Things You'll Need

Here is the list of the things you'll need to make this pizza:

  • Pizza Pan
  • Pizza Roller
  • Pizza Cutter
  • Large Bowl
  • Non-stick spray-on butter
  • Spoon
  • Cheese Grater
  • Oven
  • Plate

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Sliced Pepperoni

Making The Pizza

Now it's time to make the pizza!

  1. Pop that pizza dough out of the box or can and lay it on the pizza pan. We aren't going to make our own flour and stuff cuz its too much so we just bought stuff off of walmart or something.
  2. Grab your pizza roller and roll the dough till its flat and it can either be in a rectangle shape or a circle, whichever you prefer.
  3. Spray some of that non sticky butter on your hand and make little ridges on the edges of the pizza dough to make the crust part of the pizza.
  4. Warm up the oven to idk, however hot you want it for a bit to make the crust alittle warm or something.
  5. Get the pizza out of the oven and set it out and let it cool for a bit.
  6. Grab your spoon and get some pizza sauce with it and spread it around the pizza crust till its evenly spread. Put however much you prefer.
  7. Get your cheese grater and grate the cheese and then with that cheese you sprinkle some cheese on the pizza, like the sauce, put however much you want.
  8. Get some pepperonis and put them on the pizza. Again... put however much you want.
  9. Put the pizza in the oven and heat it up to our preferred temperature and let it sit to however you like your pizza, either cold and raw and tastes weird, or burnt and hard like a rock.
  10. Once it's cooked to how you like it, take it out of the oven, notice how I didn't list oven glove thingies, well you should've been using them to take the pizza out and not your bare hands.
  11. Now your good to go, get your pizza cutter and cut up that pizza, if you try cutting it and the cheese and stuff comes unstuck to the pizza and gets attack to your cutter, thats fine, it means you did an excellent job and now you have a loose top. I clap for you.
  12. Get your plate, big or small and get your piece a pizza. Take the biggest once because that's what everyone is after.
